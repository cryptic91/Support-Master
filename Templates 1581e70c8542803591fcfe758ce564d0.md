## How to **save configurations** while uninstalling the app

Here are the couple of steps that you need to follow to save your configurations using the development store -

1 - You have to [create a development store](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4PWeEiQ5z-o), and it is totally free.

2 - Install our app and select the subscription plan of the development store similar to your main store.

3 - You have to export and import the products using the [Shopify import/export system](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products/import-export). You have to do this for the products that are configured with our app, not all products from your store.

4 - After that, we will transfer the configurations from your main store to the development store. You have to provide us with the myShopify URLs of both stores.

Here is an example:

   Main Store: -------------------.myshopify.com
   Dev Store: -------------------.myshopify.com

5 - Then, you can uninstall the app from your main store. When you start using the main store, you will inform us, and we will copy the configurations to your main store again.

## How to use the Shopify Default Currency Converter

<details>
  <summary> ðŸ”´ </summary>

To enable the **Shopify Default Currency Converter** in your Shopify store, you need to set up local currencies. Here are the steps to do that:

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Settings**>**Markets.**
2. Click on the market where you want to enable local currencies.
3. Click **Products and Pricing**.
4. In the **Pricing** section, select the **Show prices to customers in their local currency** checkbox.
5. Click **Save**.

Once local currencies are activated, please make sure you direct your customers to the URL that matches their language and location preferences to see prices in their local currency.

</details>

## Discount takes time in Create a new product method

<details>
  <summary> ðŸŸ¢ </summary>

Hi,

Thanks for sharing the detailed information and examples regarding the issue.

When using the â€œCreate a New Productâ€ pricing type, each time a customer clicks the Add to Cart button, our app generates a new product in Shopify with the personalized options. This newly created product takes a short time [Average delay: 10â€“60 seconds] to be recognized by Shopify discount collections.

> Why the Delay Happens
> 
> 
> Shopifyâ€™s backend uses asynchronous indexing.
> 
> When you create or update a product:
> 
> - Shopify queues the product for search and collection reindexing.
> - Discounts based on collections or product conditions rely on that index.

We recommend waiting a few moments before testing the discount or switching to the Draft Order method if you need the discount to apply instantly.

Here is the detail of the Draft Order pricing method: [https://productpersonalizer.com/docs/how-options-with-costs-are-displayed-in-the-cart-checkout/#1](https://productpersonalizer.com/docs/how-options-with-costs-are-displayed-in-the-cart-checkout/#1)
